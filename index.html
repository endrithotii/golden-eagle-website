Our Story

Born in Kosovo.
Built for the World.

Founded in 1994 in Suharekë, Kosovo, Frutex began as a dream to bring world-class beverages from the heart of the Balkans. Three decades later, Golden Eagle Energy Drink soars across 35+ countries — from Europe to the USA, Australia, Asia, and Africa.

With over 200 passionate employees and a relentless drive for quality, we live by one truth: "Happiness is helping, not profiting."
